Getting dressed in the morning is one of the most important parts of the day. What you decide to wear can make your day fantastic or disasterous. That being said, it's not an easy task. Our website, Style Up,was created with this problem in mind. Style Up aims to help users get the perfect outfit for their day. It features a generator that recommends to users a large variety of different outfit combinations to revitalize their wardrobes. Users can also input the items in their closet to a virtual closet, which can then be used by the generator to curate outifts that users can pull right out of their closets. Additional features include the thrift page, which users can reference to find ways to refashion their old clothes or clothes they purchase from thrift stores.

Woman In Tech Inspiration

Amber Venz Box

Amber Venz Box, age 31, is an acclaimed Woman in Tech from Dallas, Texas. She runs a popular fashion blog called VENZedits, which she started in 2010. She also owns rewardStyle, a monetization platform for influencers, and LIKEtoKNOW.it, an app that allows users to shop from their screenshots. The young innovator made these platforms because she wanted to pave the way for fashion influencers to have more economic benefits in their competitive line of work. Some of the titles she has won are Glamour Magazine's Woman of the Year for Technology, E&Y Entrepreneur of the Year, Fashion Group International's Innovator of the Year, 2017 Forbes 30 Under 30, Business of Fashion 500, Entrepreneur Magazine's 15 Female Entrepreneurs to Watch, and Inc. Magazine's 20 Most Influential Millennials. Her work relates to our final project in that we are both trying to help people experience personal style and promote fashion in apps. We both solve the pervasive issue of “What am I going to wear today?”

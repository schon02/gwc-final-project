Thrift Flips 101





T-shirts

T-shirt are some of the easiest clothing items that you can use to transform your wardrobe. Many thrift stores offer a variety of fun t-shirts at a cheap price, which makes thrift flipping t-shirts a great way for anyone to improve their style. Check out the videos below for some inspiration!





Pants

Pants are a great way to add a subtle flair to your outfit. Thrifted jeans have the advantage of looking worn, which adds a classy vintage touch to any look.





Dresses

Dresses have the ability to bring a variety of looks to your wardrobe - from fancy and classy to flirty and casual, you can achieve many versatile looks with a dress.





Tops

Other than t-shirts, thrift stores offer many other interesting tops. Sweatshirts, jackets, long-sleeves, button-ups, and flannels are some things you can find at your local thrift store. With so many different tops to work with, the possibilities are endless!
